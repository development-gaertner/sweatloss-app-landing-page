### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**
**December 2025**

Welcome to Sweatloss! We're excited to introduce the first version of your personal hydration tracking companion. Stay healthy and build better habits by tracking your daily water intake effortlessly.

#### What's New
- **Daily Water Tracking** - Log your water intake throughout the day with quick and easy taps
- **Personalized Goals** - Set custom hydration goals based on your weight, activity level, and climate
- **Progress Insights** - View beautiful charts and statistics to track your hydration trends over time
- **Quick Logging** - Add drinks in seconds with preset serving sizes or customize your own
- **Health Benefits** - Learn about the benefits of proper hydration and its impact on wellness
- **Simple & Beautiful** - Enjoy a clean, intuitive interface that makes tracking effortless
- **Privacy Focused** - Your hydration data stays private and secure on your device. No account required
- **Multiple Beverages** - Track water, tea, coffee, and other drinks with customizable serving sizes
- **History & Stats** - Review your past hydration patterns and consistency
